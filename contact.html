Frequently Asked Questions

Are all courses really free?

Yes! All our Node.js courses and tutorials are completely free. We believe education should be accessible to everyone.

Do I need any prior programming experience?

Our beginner courses assume no prior programming experience. However, basic knowledge of JavaScript can be helpful for some intermediate courses.

How long do I have access to the courses?

You have lifetime access to all our courses. Learn at your own pace without any deadlines or time limits.

Do you provide certificates?

Yes, upon successful completion of any course, you'll receive a certificate of completion that you can add to your resume or LinkedIn profile.

Can I access courses offline?

Currently, courses require an internet connection. However, you can download course materials for offline viewing.

How do I get help with course content?

You can reach out through our contact form, join our Discord community, or post questions in our forum.
